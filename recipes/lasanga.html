Lasagna

Lasagna is a cheesy, meat-filled delight which is easily customizable to the cook's own preferences!

    Ingredients

  • 1 Pound Sweet Italian Sausage
  • 3/4 Pound Lean Ground Beef
  • 1/2 Cup Minced Onion
  • 2 Cloves Garlic, crushed
  • 1 28-Ounce Can of Crushed Tomatoes
  • 2 6.5-Ounce Cans of Tomato Sauce
  • 2 6-Ounce Cans of Tomato Paste
  • 1/2 Cup Water
  • 2 Tablespoons White Sugar
  • 4 Tablespoons Fresh Parsley
  • 1 1/2 Teaspoons Dried Basil Leaves
  • 1 1/2 Teaspoons Salt
  • 1 Teaspoons Italian Seasoning
  • 1/2 Teaspoons Fennel Seeds
  • 1/4 Teaspoons Black Pepper
  • 12 Lasagna Noodles
  • 16 Ounces Ricotta Cheese
  • 1 Egg
  • 3/4 Pound Sliced Mozarella Cheese
  • 3/4 Cup Grated Parmesean Cheese

    Steps to make Lasagna

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Cook sausage, beef, onions and garlic in oven over medium heat until well-browned.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste and water. Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds and pepper. Let it simmer and cover it for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  4. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water.
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 tablespoons parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  6. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  7. To assemble, spread 1 ½ cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce, overlapping slightly. Spread with 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 ½ cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.
  8. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray or make sure the foil does not touch the chees
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
  10. Rest lasagna for 15 minutes before serving.
